What We're Doing to Drive the Best Result
Following up with every buyer who's been through, making sure each one has had the chance to ask questions or put forward an offer before we look at next moves.
Reviewing the listing's presentation. With views strong but enquiries yet to follow, refreshing the photos could help buyers connect emotionally with the home. Styled furniture photography is the gold standard, though it comes at a higher cost and means clearing the current furniture first. Virtual staging is a faster, more affordable alternative that can achieve a similar effect, we'll talk through which option makes the most sense.
Reviewing pricing strategy together. Depending on enquiry and feedback over the next few days, we may recommend a price update, the goal being to give the market fresh hope and spark renewed competition that helps drive the result up.
